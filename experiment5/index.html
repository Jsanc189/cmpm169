Experiment 5 - 3D Graphics

Imitate

I started off with Terrain by Tiago on open processing found here: https://openprocessing.org/sketch/729536

I was able recreate the code on a p5.js editor and commented each line to make sure I understood what each piece of code did.

Integrate

From the start, I felt like the terrain looked like waves of the ocean. I made the "waves" look blue. I also managed to make the color vary depending on the noise level the shape was being formed on. Thus, the ocean changes in color from blue to sea greens

Innovate

I have been feeling very inspired by the rainy weather at the time of making this piece, so I tried to implement rain drops coming down onto the water. I had some crazy lines that kept forming, so I ended up abandoning that idea in favor of snowflakes that look like they are falling toward the viewer. From there I added a moon background for the feeling of being on a winter sea at night.

Reflection

I This project was challenging in trying to get anything to work. My rain turned into snow, and my waves were showing the background. There was a lot of putting out fires as they came up. I think for the future, I would want to figure out how to make the snowflakes look more 3D, maybe 3D shapes? I also was thinking the coloring could be darker further away from the moon, and have the moons reflection underneath it in the water.
